About Patterson Pump Singapore

One of the world's leading suppliers of quality pumps for the Fire, Municipal, Industrial, Flood Control, Plumbing, and HVAC Markets. Patterson Pump Company offers an extensive line of products that serve customers worldwide.

Established on 1st May 2011, Patterson Pump Singapore was set up to establish a distribution network in the Far East Markets. We have distributors in:

  • Australia
  • Brunei
  • China
  • Hong Kong
  • Indonesia
  • Malaysia
  • Maldives
  • Myanmar
  • Singapore
  • Sri Lanka
  • Vietnam

All our distributors have undergone thorough training on NFPA20. Not only can they provide technical advice, but also after-sales services with their respective start-up engineers. The accumulated years of experiences of our distributors, on Fire Fighting Pumps specifically, are more than 300 years and have supplied hundreds of fire pumps to Residential, Industrial, Oil & Gas, and Power Plants, protecting lives and the facilities.

Since our inception in 2011, we have delivered an average of 200 orders a year in pumps and parts.
